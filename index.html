Welcome to Inteva

Based in the North West of England, Inteva are a long established event production company who work on a national and international basis. Our aim is to be at the heart of your event, working with you to ensure that its key messages are delivered with clarity and understanding, in a way that will have the greatest impact on your audience.

Production Equipment

Audio

With every venue being different we can supply a professional quality audio system to provide you with effective sound reinforcement to suit any event.

Lighting

We have a large selection of lighting equipment to illuminate your event, from generic lighting and LEDs to intelligent lighting systems. Give your event audience impact.

Video

Whether you need live images on screen or you need to record your event for the future, we stock a range of HD/SD cameras to suit your needs.

Projection

Our range of projectors and screens will provide the audience all they will need to view your presentation. From large multi screen events, to small meetings and breakout rooms.

Stage

Our light weight versatile staging will lift your event to perfection, with its clean lines and futuristic design the system has various styles, combinations and heights.

Scenery

Bespoke set designs or a choice from a wide variety of stock panels, we can offer you the perfect back drop to your event.

As every event is different please call us or use the the Contact Us page to let us know your requirements so that we can give you a price specific to your requirements.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to work in partnership with our clients, to use our experience and expertise to help them communicate more effectively.

With over fifty years of collective experience in staging events, you can rest assured that with Inteva, you will be in safe hands. Throughout the years we have worked with a diverse range of clients, from small local businesses to multi-national corporations, providing a variety of services, from the hire of a single microphone to a full event production and management service.
